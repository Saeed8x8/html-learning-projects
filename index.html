Why Football is famous worldwide ?

I bet you all are wondering why football is so famous when all you
got to do is kick a football in a goal outpost?
The answer to that question winds up to one word "skill".
All the players are so much skill in their art and respective positions
That hitting a ball in a huge outpost becomes almost impossible
that sometimes matches end 0-0.
That what makes the game so much interesting hence also the
reason it is famous worldwide.
